Nutritional Levels

Understand your body’s nutritional needs for a healthier life

What are Nutritional Levels?

Nutritional levels refer to the balance of essential nutrients your body needs to function properly. Maintaining correct nutritional levels improves immunity, energy, metabolism, and overall well-being.

Macronutrients

Carbohydrates

Main source of energy. Choose complex carbs like whole grains, fruits, and vegetables.

Proteins

Essential for muscle growth, repair, and immunity. Sources include pulses, eggs, fish, and dairy.

Healthy Fats

Supports brain function and hormone balance. Found in nuts, seeds, and olive oil.

Micronutrients

Vitamins

Boost immunity and energy levels. Important vitamins include A, B, C, D, and E.

Minerals

Essential for bones, nerves, and muscles. Includes calcium, iron, zinc, and magnesium.

Maintain Healthy Nutritional Levels

  • ✔ Eat a balanced diet daily
  • ✔ Drink enough water
  • ✔ Avoid excessive junk food
  • ✔ Include fruits & vegetables
  • ✔ Exercise regularly
